Who are we?

Our book club has started since 4/12/2021 eating gelato with three members.

There are three rules for our book club.
1. Read at least one book in a month.
2. Participate our webinar which is hosted once a week.
3. Whoever breaks the rules above, take the others out for lunch/dinner.

Our ultimate goal is to be a book-friendly person.
